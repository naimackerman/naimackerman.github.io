About

I graduated in Informatics Engineering from Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology (ITS) in Surabaya. I worked at DataOn (HRIS, 2023–2025), contributed remotely to the Stanford Intelligent Systems Laboratory (SISL), and freelanced for Suitmedia (2022–2023). Along the way I taught data structures and ran trainings for the student association. I'm working toward becoming a full-time researcher in clinical AI.
